About Us

Our Mission

At the University of Virginia, we are on a mission to create a sustainable and eco-friendly campus environment. We believe that every small step towards energy conservation can make a significant impact on our planet's future. That's why we've introduced the "Green Rivalry: The Dorm Energy Challenge."

What We Do

  • Promoting Sustainability: We organize and manage an exciting competition among dorms to reduce their energy consumption. This competition encourages students to embrace sustainable living practices and make a positive change in our community.

  • Tracking Progress: Our state-of-the-art monitoring systems track the energy usage of participating dorms in real-time. This data helps us create leaderboards and graphs that showcase the efforts of our dorm residents.

  • Rewarding Excellence: To incentivize sustainability, we offer substantial rewards for the dorm that excels in the competition. The winning dorm receives a generous prize of $20,000, along with the satisfaction of leading the way towards a greener future.

Why Sustainability Matters

We firmly believe that sustainability is not just a buzzword; it's a responsibility. By reducing energy consumption, we contribute to a cleaner environment, lower energy costs, and a healthier planet for future generations. Sustainability is at the core of our values, and we are committed to spreading awareness and action among the UVA's community.

Join Us!

Are you passionate about sustainability? Do you want to make a difference on campus and beyond? Join us in the "Green Rivalry: The Dorm Energy Challenge." Whether you're a dorm resident looking to compete or a volunteer interested in supporting our mission, there's a place for you on our team.
